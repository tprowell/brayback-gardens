# Barn Garden — Layout Specification

## Overall Dimensions
- **Width:** 45 feet
- **Depth:** 80 feet 1 inch (80.083 ft)
- **Total area:** ~3,604 sq ft
- **Grid scale:** 1 square = 2 ft (from original graph paper drawing)

## Coordinate System
All coordinates use the **bottom-left corner as origin (0, 0)**, with x increasing to the right and y increasing upward. This matches the physical garden when standing at the outer fence looking in.

## Boundaries

### Outer Fence (Bottom Edge)
- Runs the full 80'1" width along y = 0
- This is the primary perimeter fence

### Other Edges
- **Left edge:** solid boundary, 45 ft tall (x = 0)
- **Top edge:** solid boundary, 80'1" wide (y = 45)
- **Right edge:** solid boundary with a gate opening (x = 80'1")

## Gates

### Fence Gate
- **Location:** bottom edge (outer fence), centered at approximately x = 38 ft
- **Width:** 4 ft 1 in (4.083 ft)
- **Type:** opening in the outer fence

### Main Entry Gate
- **Location:** right edge (x = 80'1")
- **Opening:** 4 ft 3 in (4.25 ft)
- **Position on right edge from bottom:** 7 ft 9 in (7.75 ft) of solid boundary, then the 4'3" gate opening, then 32 ft of solid boundary to the top
  - Gate bottom y = 7.75 ft
  - Gate top y = 12.0 ft

## Water Spigots

## Greenhouse
- **Dimensions:** 10 ft x 8 ft
- **Position (bottom-left corner):** x = 57 ft, y = 28.4 ft
- **Distance from right edge:** 12 ft 5 in (12.417 ft) from the greenhouse's right side to the garden's right boundary
- **Distance from top edge:** 6 ft 7 in (6.583 ft) from the greenhouse's top side to the garden's top boundary
- **Step/apron:** 2 ft 8 in (2.667 ft) extending from one side of the greenhouse
